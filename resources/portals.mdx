## General Resources

### myBYUI

- **URL:** https://my.byui.edu/
- **Users:** Students, Faculty, Staff, Admin
- **Keywords:** portal, student, pathify, dashboard, shortcuts

myBYUI (my.byui.edu) is the official digital portal for Brigham Young University-Idaho, serving as a one-stop resource for campus events, announcements, tasks, shortcuts, tools, and more.

### Announcements

- **URL:** https://my.byui.edu/announcements
- **Users:** Students, Faculty, Staff
- **Keywords:** announcements, news, updates, notifications, alerts

View official university announcements, news updates, and important notifications for the BYU-Idaho community.

### Events & Calendars

- **URL:** https://my.byui.edu/events
- **Users:** Students, Faculty, Staff
- **Keywords:** events, calendar, activities, schedule, campus events

Browse upcoming campus events, activities, and important dates. Filter by category and add events to your personal calendar.

### Campus Account

- **URL:** https://web.byui.edu/account/
- **Users:** Students, Faculty, Staff
- **Keywords:** account, username, password, credentials, login, reset password

Manage your campus account credentials. Reset your local username and password, update security settings, and manage account access.

Access official BYU-Idaho policies, guidelines, and procedures. Reference university standards and compliance requirements.

### Workday

- **URL:** https://wd501.myworkday.com/byui/
- **Users:** Faculty, Staff, Employees
- **Keywords:** payroll, HR, benefits, time off, expenses, pay stub, PTO, leave

Human resources and payroll system. View pay stubs, submit time-off requests, update personal/tax info, access benefits enrollment, and submit expense reports.

 Note: On January 2026, as part of the Workday divestiture, BYU-Idaho transitioned to a new Workday tenant. Employees will need to use the new url to access the new tenant. Employees using the Workday mobile app will need to delete the app, reinstall it, and then use “byui” as the new company code when signing in.

**For reference**:  
Old Url: `https://www.myworkday.com/byuhi/d/home.htmld`  
New Url: `https://wd501.myworkday.com/byui/`

Employees can access training materials for the new Workday tenant by visiting `https://www.byui.edu/workday/workday-training` or learn more about the divestiture by visiting `https://www.byui.edu/workday/divestiture`
